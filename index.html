Nostril-Specific Breathing & Heart Rate Variability

Participant Consent

This study investigates the effects of pranayama (nostril-specific breathing) on autonomic nervous system function, measured via remote photoplethysmography (rPPG) from your webcam.

  • Your webcam will be used to measure heart rate and HRV. No video is stored.
  • Only numerical HRV values and questionnaire scores are saved.
  • Your data is stored securely and used for research purposes only.
  • You may stop at any time.
